Neural Dynamics

Understanding neural populations as interacting dynamical systems.

Overview

We study neural populations using dynamical systems theory, statistical inference, and network analysis. Our work focuses on extracting functional connectivity and collective behaviour from large-scale neural recordings such as Neuropixels datasets. We aim to understand how coordinated activity emerges from interacting neurons and how such structure relates to information processing.
