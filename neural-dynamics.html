Neural Dynamics

Understanding neural populations as interacting dynamical systems.

Overview

We study neural populations using dynamical systems theory, statistical inference, and network analysis. Our work focuses on interpreting networks of neurons as complex systems depicting rich behaviour. We use tools from nonlinear analysis, time series analysis, network theory and pattern formation to investigate these systems.
